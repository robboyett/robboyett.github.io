AI in design, tool and model release timeline 2022–2025

2022
Foundation model
OpenAI DALL·E 2
April 2022
Breakthrough text-to-image generation with much higher realism and resolution. Catalysed the AI art wave and demonstrated creative potential to a broad audience, inspiring competitors and prompting incumbents to invest in generative imaging.
Catalyzed the AI art wave: Demonstrated the creative potential of AI to a broad audience. Sparked numerous image-gen tools (many early private betas used DALL·E 2 API). Inspired competitors (Imagen, Stable Diffusion) and prompted incumbents (Adobe) to invest in generative imaging. Though access was limited at first, by late 2022 its capabilities influenced design workflows (designers began using AI for moodboards, concept art).
Development tool
GitHub Copilot
June 2022
AI code assistant powered by OpenAI Codex. Auto-completes code and suggests functions from natural-language comments, lowering the barrier for designers to build interactive prototypes and handle repetitive code tasks.
Highly influential: Although primarily for coding, Copilot impacted design prototyping. Launched (powered by OpenAI Codex) as an IDE extension in 2022, it auto-completes code and suggests functions from natural-language comments . It quickly gained hundreds of thousands of users, showing the viability of AI pair-programmers. For designers, Copilot lowered the barrier to build interactive prototypes and handle repetitive code tasks. There was some controversy over code licensing, but overall reception was enthusiastic.
Image generation
Midjourney
July 2022
High-quality AI art generator launched via Discord with vibrant community. Known for signature illustrative style and detail, becoming the go-to tool for designers seeking concept visuals and creative inspiration.
Foundation model
Stable Diffusion 1.0
August 2022
First open-source image AI model at commercial quality. Enabled a wave of custom models, plugins, and indie design apps due to its open availability, essentially commoditising image generation for every designer and app.
Foundation model
OpenAI Whisper
September 2022
High-accuracy speech-to-text transcription, open-sourced. Revolutionised audio and video design by making instant transcripts of meetings, interviews, and videos trivial to generate across multiple languages.
Design platform
Canva “Magic Write”
December 2022
GPT-powered writing inside Canva Docs; the first “Magic” feature that primed the broader Magic suite launch in March 2023.
Foundation model
OpenAI GPT-3.5 (ChatGPT)
November 2022
Conversational AI with unprecedented fluency. Reached 100 million users in 2 months and mainstreamed generative AI, directly enabling AI copilots and design assistants across productivity software.
2023
Foundation model
Meta LLaMA 1
February 2023
Open-access large language model that spawned an ecosystem of custom local chatbots and AI features. Enabled specialised design advisors and AI features embedded in software that could run privately.
Foundation model
Adobe Firefly
March 2023
Commercial-safe image generation trained on licensed content. Users generated over 100 million assets in the first six weeks, addressing legal concerns whilst enabling commercial use across Creative Cloud applications.
Foundation model
OpenAI GPT-4
March 2023
Multimodal AI accepting images with significantly improved reasoning and coding abilities. Enabled advanced AI design assistants and visual assistants that could analyse UI screenshots or generate HTML from hand-drawn mockups.
Foundation model
Baidu ERNIE Bot
March 2023
Chinese large language model optimised for Mandarin and Chinese cultural context. Spurred widespread AI integration in Chinese apps and design platforms, enabling native-language creative assistance for Chinese designers.
Video generation
Runway Gen-2
March 2023
First publicly available text-to-video generator capable of creating 4-second video clips from text prompts. Also enabled video-to-video transformation, used by creators for prototyping visuals.
Design platform
Canva Magic Suite
March 2023
Comprehensive AI features including Magic Write, Magic Design, and Magic Edit. Reached Canva's massive user base with millions embracing these tools for quick content creation and template generation.
Foundation model
Anthropic Claude
March 2023
Large-context conversational AI emphasising safety and reliability. Claude's large context window enabled document analysis and design knowledge bases, becoming the brain behind many professional productivity tools.
Computer vision
Meta Segment Anything Model (SAM)
April 2023
Universal image segmentation model able to select any object in an image with a prompt or click. Quickly found its way into design pipelines through Figma plugins and Photoshop scripts for instant cutouts.
Instant cutouts & mask generation: SAM quickly found its way into design pipelines – e.g. Figma plugins and Photoshop scripts appeared that let users click an element in an image to isolate or replace it using SAM’s masks. This saved designers tedious manual work when creating product mockups or composites. SAM also empowered AR/VR design (segmenting scene elements in real time) and influenced other tools (Adobe’s Remove Background improved in 2023 likely using similar tech). Essentially, SAM became the “background removal on steroids” service in many apps. It underscored how foundational CV models (even non-generative ones) can dramatically speed up graphic design tasks.
Design platform
Adobe Generative Fill
May 2023
Brought text-prompt image edits directly into Photoshop. Over 100 million generative edits made in months, hailed as a "magic" productivity boost enabling designers to create and ideate faster within familiar tools.
By late 2023 – Now fully integrated in Photoshop for all users. Continues to evolve (Adobe released Firefly 2 in 2023 to improve quality). Widely adopted by photographers and designers; no retractions, only expansions.
Collaboration tool
Miro AI
May 2023
12-feature suite including sticky-note clustering, summarising, image generation, and mind-map expansion. Enterprise users found it helpful for speeding up workshops and auto-summarising research notes.
Web design
Framer "Start with AI"
June 2023
AI feature generating multi-page websites from text prompts. Initially generated buzz for rapid prototyping, though outputs were template-like. Later deprioritised as Framer refocused on core design capabilities.
Foundation model
Meta LLaMA 2
July 2023
Open-source chat LLM with commercial-friendly license. Enabled startups and incumbents to embed capable AI directly into apps without API costs, accelerating AI features in design software.
Foundation model
Anthropic Claude 2
July 2023
Expanded context (~100k+ tokens) and reliability improvements, enabling long-form spec and research analysis in design workflows.
Development tool
Cursor AI IDE
October 2023
VS Code fork augmented with AI offering code autocompletion, natural-language edits, and "agent" mode. Tailored for developers to build software faster with comprehensive AI support throughout the coding process.
UI generation
v0 by Vercel
October 2023
"Describe and deploy" website builder allowing designers and non-coders to create full-stack applications from prompts. Unique for combining frontend, backend, and deployment in one AI tool.
UI generation
Galileo AI
October 2023
"ChatGPT for interface design" generating editable UI mockups from text in seconds. Pioneered text-to-multiscreen UI generation, accelerating product design workflows before being acquired by Google.
Foundation model
Google Gemini
December 2023
Multimodal foundation model handling text, images, and more. Became the engine behind Google's design-related products and paved the way for unified design assistants that can chat about drawings or generate UI code from sketches.
2024
UI generation
Galileo AI public beta
February 2024
Public beta launch following $4.4M seed funding. Gained widespread attention through viral demo posts showcasing rapid UI generation capabilities before eventual acquisition by Google.
Design platform
Figma AI features
June 2024
Suite of AI tools including visual/asset search, auto layout suggestions, and interface generation from prompts. Cautious waitlist-based rollout with positive reception in beta, helping designers work faster.
App builder
Lovable AI launch
November 2024
#1 Product Hunt launch. AI "full-stack engineer" enabling users to chat and build web apps with UI, code, and database. GPT-based "AI teammate" can plan, code, and deploy apps from natural language.
2025
Investment
Cursor Series B
January 2025
Raised $105M Series B at $2.5B valuation. Continuing rapid expansion as standalone product with growing user base, demonstrating strong market demand for AI-enhanced development environments.
Investment
Lovable AI Series A
February 2025
Raised $15M following rapid growth to 500,000 users. Continuing as independent platform with high ARR growth, validating the no-code AI development approach for mainstream users.
Acquisition
Google acquires Galileo AI
May 2025
Google acquired Galileo, rebranding it as "Stitch" and integrating with Gemini models. Signals major tech companies' commitment to dominating the AI-powered design tool landscape.
Design platform
Figma Make
7 May 2025
Prompt-to-app tool announced at Config 2025 to turn ideas or Figma files into working prototypes and web apps via conversation. Reached general availability on 24 July 2025 with Supabase integration for auth, data and APIs.
Foundation model
OpenAI GPT-5 (ChatGPT-5)
7 August 2025
Next-gen unified model with stronger reasoning, coding and multimodal performance; rolled into ChatGPT as the new top-tier model.
Image generation
Google “Nano Banana” (Gemini image editing)
26 August 2025
New Gemini image-editing model focused on fast, high-quality edits and blends inside the Gemini app; positioned as an upgrade to Gemini’s image tools.
