Tool and model release timeline 2022–2025

epoch
Foundation model breakthroughs
2021–2022
The era of breakthrough models like GPT-3, DALL·E 2, and Stable Diffusion that demonstrated unprecedented creative capabilities and sparked mainstream AI adoption.
2021
foundation model
OpenAI DALL·E 2
April 2022
Breakthrough text-to-image generation with much higher realism and resolution. Catalysed the AI art wave and demonstrated creative potential to a broad audience, inspiring competitors and prompting incumbents to invest in generative imaging.
Catalyzed the AI art wave: Demonstrated the creative potential of AI to a broad audience. Sparked numerous image-gen tools (many early private betas used DALL·E 2 API). Inspired competitors (Imagen, Stable Diffusion) and prompted incumbents (Adobe) to invest in generative imaging. Though access was limited at first, by late 2022 its capabilities influenced design workflows (designers began using AI for moodboards, concept art).
