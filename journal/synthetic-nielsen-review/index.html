Synthetic Jakob Nielsen Reviews Your Site

Using Claude to control Chrome and run heuristic evaluations

I've been experimenting with different ways to use LLMs to evaluate built UI, sometimes at the level of a single page, but ideally across full flows. The goal is two-part: quick review of what's been launched to find things to improve, and to strengthen my prototyping work earlier by catching issues around heuristics, accessibility, and general quality before moving to human-based testing.

Today I realised Claude desktop can speak to and control Chrome.

So I set up a quick test: synthetic "Jakob Nielsen" stepping through the Anthropic site to a specific article.

The results are surprisingly useful, if a little long.
